Large banks do not have a simple onboarding problem; they have a controlled-access orchestration problem. Every new joiner needs to become productive quickly, but every access decision must be justified, approved, traceable, and compliant. OnboardAI uses specialised AI agents to convert a new-joiner event into a dynamic onboarding journey. It recommends the right access, detects missing information, routes approvals, blocks risky access, explains decisions with policy citations, and generates an audit-ready evidence pack. The result is faster day-one productivity with stronger zero-trust governance.
